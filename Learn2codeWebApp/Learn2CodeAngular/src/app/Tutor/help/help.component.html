Hi, how can we help?



View your profile?

Purpose: To View the profile of the Tutor who is logged in.

How to: Log in to the system, the system will display the tutor home page where you will see your profile and its details which are displayed on a card.

Edit your profile?

Purpose: For the tutor to edit their personal information on the system.

How to: Select the edit button on the profile card on the tutor homepage, this causes a pop-up to appear with all the appropriate input fields for the tutor to enter new information. Then proceed to select the update button and thereafter the yes button on the confirmation pop-up in order to update your profile.

Delete your profile?

Purpose: To delete your profile from the system.

How to: Select the delete button on profile card on the tutor homepage, this causes a confirmation pop-up to appear. Proceed to confirm the deletion of your profile by selecting the yes button. If you whish to abort the deletion click the cancel button.


Sending a message?

Purpose: To send a message to a specific student.

How to: Navigate to the home page and clcik on the student icon on the sidebar. The system will display all students, simply click on the message icon relating to the student you wish to send a message to on the students screen. A pop up will display asking for your message, provide the message and click send. The system will prompt a confirmation.

Your messages?

Purpose: To find a specific message that you have received/sent.

How to: Navigate to the My Messages screen using the side navigation bar and clicks on the Messages icon. The system will display all the my messages screen with a list of messages related to you. Either click on the recieved tab to see recieved messages or the sent tab to see sent messages

Delete your messages?

Purpose:To delete a sent message from the system.

How to: Proceed to select the sent tab and select the delete button of the message you want to delete. This will cause a confirmation message to pop-up where the student can confirm the deletion. If the message was sent less that 24 hours ago you will be unable to delete the message.


Change Password?

Purpose:To change your password once logged in.

How to: Proceed to click the change password button on your profile card. Then fill in your new password details in the appropriate input fields on the pop-up. Inorder to save the new password click the submit button and the system will prompt confirmation. In turn to confirm you must click the yes button.

Reset password?

Purpose:To allow the user to reset their password using their email address.

How to: On the login page click the “forgot password” hyperlink. Proceed to enter yor email in the space provided. The system will then send an email providing a reset link. Follow the link and provide a new password and click the submit button.

Logout?

Purpose: To logout of the Learn2Code system.

How to: Navigate to the dropdown menu by clicking the profile picture icon on the far right of the top navigation bar. Then proceed to click the logout button.


Sessions?

Purpose:to explain sessions.

How to: A tutor can create a number of sessions during the week as long as they do not clash with existing sessions. You as a tutor can either create individual sessions which are one on one sessions or group sessions which are with multiple students. The sessions you create are based on the specific modules that you are registered for.

Creating sessions?

Purpose: To allow the tutor a create a new session.

How to: Navigate to the my schedule screen by clicking the sessions icon on the side navbar, clicks on the my sessions tab and then on the new session button. Then fill in and select the appropriate session information and selects the create button. The system will prompt confirmation and provide it by clicking the yes button.

My sessions?

Purpose:Allows the tutor to view their sessions.

How to: Navigate to the my schedule screen and click on the my sessions tab. The system will display a lsit of all your sessions, alternatively click on the calendar tab to see all your sessinons on a calendar.


Update sessions?

Purpose:Let a tutor edit their sessions.

How to: Navigate to the my schedule screen, click on the my sessions tab and then on the edit button on the specific session. Then edit and select the appropriate session information and select the create button. The system will prompt confirmation and click the yes button on the alert. Sessions can't be updated 24 hours before they start.

Delete sessions?

Purpose:Deleting a session that has been created.

How to: Navigates to the my schedule screen, click on the my sessions tab and then on the delete button on the specific session. The system will prompt confirmation and the tutor will provide it by clicking the yes button on the alert. Sessions can't be deleted 24 hours before they start.

Finalize sessions?

Purpose:The finalization of a group session.

How to: Navigate to the my schedule screen, click on the my sessions tab and then select the finalize button for the specific session you want to finalize. This will cause a pop-up to appear where the take attendance button and the upload content button will be enabled, these 2 functions need to be done in order for the finalize button to be enabled.


Take attendance?

Purpose:To take register for a group session.

How to: Navigate to the attendance screen by either the home side navigation bar by clicking the attendance icon. Click the "+" button for a specific session to take attendance and proceed to check all the students that attended. Click the submit button to save.

Update attendance?

Purpose To edit the attendance list of students.

How to: Navigate to the attendance screen by either the home side navigation bar by clicking the attendance icon. Click the edit button on the specific session, then click the check-box next to the for the students that attended and leave the check-boxes blank next to those who didn't attend the session. Then click the submit button to save.

Delete attendance?

Purpose To delete the attendance list of students that attend the group sessions.

How to: Navigate to the attendance screen by either the home side navigation bar by clicking the attendance icon. Then click the delete button on the specific session. The system will prompt confirmation, confirm by clicking the yes button.


Create resource category?

Purpose: To add a resource category to the system.

How to: Navigate to the resources screen from the side nav bar by clicking the resources icon, click on the resource categories tab and then on the add category button. Then add a new category name into the input field on the pop-up and select the submit button. This causes a confirmation message to appear. Select yes to confirm

Update resource category?

Purpose: To update a resource category name.

How to: Navigates to the resources screen from the side navigation bar on the tutorhome, click on the resource categories tab and then on the edit button of the specific resource category you want to edit. A pop-up appears where you can make all changes to the category name and submit the changes. A confirmation pop-up will appear. click yes to save.

Delete resource category?

Purpose:To be able to delete the resource category.

How to: Navigates to the resources screen from the side navigation bar on the tutorhome, click on the resource categories tab and then on the delete button of the specific resource category you want to delete. A confirmation pop-up appears, then confirm the deletion by selecting the yes button.


Create resource?

Purpose: To add a resource file to a module.

How to: Navigate to the resources screen by using the side navigation bar on home screen. Then click on the resources button on a specific module card. Proceed to click on the add resource button and provide the content that the pop-up prompts. Then clicks the submit button. The system will prompt confirmation and click the yes button to confirm.

Edit resource?

Purpose: To edit a resource belonging to a module.

How to: Navigates to the resources screen from the side navigation bar on the tutorhome, click on the resource categories tab and then on the edit button of the specific resource category you want to edit. A pop-up appears where you can make all changes to the category name and submit the changes. A confirmation pop-up will appear. click yes to save.

Delete resources?

Purpose: To remove resources belonging to a module.

How to: Navigate to the resources screen by using the side navigation bar on the tutor home screen. Then click on the resources button on a specific module card, then click on the delete button. The system will prompt confirmation and you will confirm by clicking the yes button.


Create session content?

Purpose: To add content for group session.

How to: Navigates to the specific session content screen by either the home side navigation bar and selecting the appropriate session you want to add content. Thereafter select the add content button and a pop-up appears where the appropriate content type is selected and the video and notes files are added.

Update session content?

Purpose: To edit content for a group session.

How to: Navigates to the specific session content screen by either the home side navigation bar and selecting the appropriate session you want to edit content for. Thereafter select the edit button and a pop-up appears where the appropriate content type is selected and the video and notes files are added.

Delete session content?

Purpose:Remove specific content for a group session.

How to: Navigates to the specific session content screen by either the home side navigation bar and selecting the appropriate session you want to edit content for, then click the delete button. The system will prompt confirmation, confirm by clicking the yes button.
