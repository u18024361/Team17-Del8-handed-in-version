Hi, how can we help?



View your profile?

Purpose: To View the profile of the student who is logged in.

How to: Log in to the system, proceed to the navigation bar at the top of the screen and click the circle with your initial in it. A drop down will apear with two options "logout" and "profile", select the profile option. The system will display your Profile

Edit your profile?

Purpose: To edit the profile of the student who is logged in.

How to: navigate to your profile by using the navigation bar. Then proceed to click the "edit" button. A pop up will display asking for the edited details, provide all the required details and click the "update" button. The system will then prompt for confirmation.

Delete your profile?

Purpose: To remove the students profile from the Learn2Code system.

How to: Navigate to your profile by using the navigation bar. Proceed to click the "delete" button on the profile page. The system will then prompt for confirmation.If you wish to confirm click the "ok" button in the confirmation otherwise click the "cancel" button.


Sending a message?

Purpose: To send a message to a specific tutor.

How to: Navigate to the home page and clcik on the tutors icon on the sidebar. The system will display all the tutors, simply click on the message icon relating to the tutor you wish to send a message to on the tutors screen. A pop up will display asking for your message, provide the message and click send. The system will prompt a confirmation.

Your messages?

Purpose: To find a specific message that you have received/sent.

How to: Navigate to the My Messages screen using the side navigation bar and clicks on the Messages icon. The system will display all the my messages screen with a list of messages related to you. Either click on the recieved tab to see recieved messages or the sent tab to see sent messages

Delete your messages?

Purpose:To delete a sent message from the system.

How to: Proceed to select the sent tab and select the delete button of the message you want to delete. This will cause a confirmation message to pop-up where the student can confirm the deletion. If the message was sent less that 24 hours ago you will be unable to delete the message.


Add to basket?

Purpose:To add an item from the shop into the cart so it can then be purchased.

How to: Navigates to the Shop screen using the side navigation bar on the home screen. For subscriptions select a module in the dropdown and same with short courses select an option in the dropdown. then select which items you wish to purchase by clicking the Buy button on the card.

Remove basket items?

Purpose:To remove an unwanted item from the shopping cart.

How to: Navigates to the Cart drop down by clicking on the cart icon in the top navigation bar on the shop screen. Select which items you wish to remove by clicking the “x” icon button relating to a specific instance in the cart. The system will remove that item from your cart.

Checkout?

Purpose:To make a payment for the the cart.

How to: Navigate to the Cart drop down by clicking on the cart icon in the navigation bar. The student then clicks the checkout button and then the system prompts for confirmation and the student gives it by clicking the yes button on the pop-up which diverts the student to Paystack payment gateway.


Create feedback?

Purpose:to provide feedback on past sessions.

How to: Navigates to the Past Session Feedback screen using the side navigation bar on the student home screen. he student then selects which session they wish to provide feedback for by clicking the plus button on the specific session instance. Then provided all their feedback details in the input fields provided and click submit.

My feedback?

Purpose:To allow students to find their feedback.

How to: Navigates to the Past Session Feedback screen using the side navigation bar on the student home screen. Then click the My Feedback tab and then provide the search criteria of their choosing in the search input field provided. In turn the system will display your feedback that relates to the search criteria

Delete feedback?

Purpose:To allow students to remove feedback.

How to: Navigate to the Past Session Feedback screen using the side navigation bar on the student home screen. Then click the My Feedback tab and locate the specific instance you wish to delete, Then proceed to click the delete button. The system prompts for confirmation and select the yes option to confirm.


Group Sessions?

Purpose:Explenation on group sessions.

How to: Group sessions are sessions that consist of multiple students and one tutor. They are based on a specific module and happen weekly based on the schedule of the tutor. In order to be part of group session you must buy group tickets for a module in the shop. Each time the tutor creates a session you will be invited and loose a ticket.

My group sessions?

Purpose:To allow the viewing of group sessions.

How to: Navigate to the home screen and click on the group session icon in the side nav bar. The system will redirect you to the group session screen where a list of your upcoming group sessions will be displayed.

Group session content?

Purpose:To allow the viewing of session content.

How to: Navigate to the home screen and click on the group session content icon in the side nav bar. The system will redirect you to the group session content screen where a list of your past group sessions will be displayed. Click on the content button of a specific session to view its uploaded content.


Individual session?

Purpose:Explenation on group session.

How to: Group sessions are sessions that consist of one students and one tutor. They are based on a specific module and happen weekly based on the schedule of the tutor. In order to be part of Individual session you must buy individual tickets for a module in the shop. In turn you will have to book a session which rsults in the loss of a ticket.

My individual session?

Purpose Viewing of your individual sessions.

How to: Navigate to the home screen and click on the Individual session icon in the side nav bar. The system will redirect you to the individual session screen click the my sessions tab and a list of your upcoming individual sessions will be displayed.

Edit individual session?

Purpose Updationg your individual sessions.

How to: On the individual session click the edit button of the specific session you want to edit. Provide all the required details and submit. The system will send an email to the tutor and depending on your request the tutor can choose to update the booking. Updates cant be made if the session is less than 24 hours away.


Delete individual session?

Purpose deleting your individual sessions.

How to: On the individual session click the delete button of the specific session you want to delete. The system will prompt for confirmation, click the yes button to confrim. On successfull deletion you will be refunded your ticket. Deletion cannot take place 24 hours before a session will start.

Book individual session?

Purpose Book an individual session.

How to: On the individual session clicki the shedule tab, in the drop down select a module that you have purshed tickets for and then click the search button. The system will display availabe slots for that module. Proceed to click the book button of a specific slot and provide the relevant details. The system will prompt for confirmation.

Tickets?

Purpose Explenation on tickets.

How to: Tickets are purchasabel through the store for a specific module. Tickets allow you to either attend a group session or book an individual session, depending on the type of ticket. Tickets do have an expiery date which you can view by navigarting to the my subscriptions tab in the side bar of the home screen.


Short courses?

Purpose Explenation on short courses.

How to: Short courses are products taht you can buy in the store that relate to a specific topic. Short courses consist of a video on a topic and any other resources such as notes for that specific topic.

My short courses?

Purpose How to view your short courses.

How to: Navigate to the home page, if you have bough a short course you will see a card displaying the details of that short course. Proceed to click on the learn button for that specific course. You will be directed to the content page where you can view the recources for that short course.

Resources?

Purpose How to view Resources.

How to: Navigate to the home page and click on the resources icon. The system will redirect you to the resources page where you can select a module and search for it resources. Resources are uploaded by a tutor and consist of practice tests, notes or articles.
